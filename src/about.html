About Us

IEEE is a professional association whose objectives are the educational and technical advancement of electrical and electronics engineering, computer engineering, and other related disciplines. As a national institution, IEEE publishes literature, hosts conferences, offers educational programs, and recognizes contributions to the field of electrical and computer engineering that advance the field.

At the university level, we work to expose ECE students at Pitt to what IEEE has to offer and provide a helpful environment in which students can learn more about the field. We work to connect students with employers through various professional events, enhance our members’ learning experiences through hands on workshops and a tutoring network, and provide a social outlet for peers to get to know one another.

Mission Statement

IEEE’s core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

Goals

  • Provide professional events throughout the year to connect current students with employers, in the form of information sessions, professional dinners, career panels, and other networking events
  • Provide hands-on workshops throughout the year to expose members to new and important experiences
  • Provide a network of tutors that can help younger students within the ECE program in their undergraduate classes
  • Provide social events in which ECE peers can get to know each other better
  • Create a sense of unity within the department
  • Ensure that all professors and faculty are actively involved in students' education
