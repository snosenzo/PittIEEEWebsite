Sponsorship

As a student organization, it is our goal to provide our members/peers, an opportunity to explore a wide variety of topics within our field, allow them to gain special skills that will help them in their career, and most importantly, give them a chance to network with not only their classmates, but with local companies that could be their future employer. With your sponsorship, we will be able to hold social events to bring our members together, we will be able to hold workshops that can introduce or even strengthen their skillset, and we will be able to host networking events where your company can speak with our members and see what the University of Pittsburgh has to offer. Sponsorship does not necessarily mean only monetary contributions, an information or site visit gives our members the opportunity to learn more about your company. Thank you for your interest and we hope to see you involved!

How to Sponsor

Based on the sponsorship opportunities, you choose what you'd like to sponsor and how much. Just choose your method of donation and follow the steps below:

Online:   Please go to www.giveto.pitt.edu and click "GIVE NOW", then put "IEEE" for "Other Organization".

By Mail:   After printing and completeing the form below, mail it to this address:
